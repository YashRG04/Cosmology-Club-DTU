Faculty Advisor

Prof. Rinku Sharma
H.O.D. Physics

Prof. Rinku Sharma, the HOD of the Applied Physics Department, Delhi Technological University and the major faculty advisor of our society, the “Cosmology Club of DTU” has been the backbone for us through and through. She has been the pillar without which we would never have reached the heights that we have achieved by this date. With almost ten years of teaching experience at DTU under her belt, she is one of the most invaluable assets for the college which is evident from her being the HOD of Applied Physics. Apart from that, when it concerns to our society, she has been the person to look up to when in need in any kind of help and she has succeeded in providing it to us without fail. We are extremely grateful and indebted towards her for being there for us, as our faculty advisor, as our mentor and without doubt as our guiding light.
